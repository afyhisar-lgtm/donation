Donation Terms of Service

Payment Commitment: You authorize Raindrop Dallas to charge your selected payment method (Card or ACH) on a monthly basis starting on your selected Start Date.

1. Fiscal Year End & Cancellation

Unless "Auto-renew" is selected, your subscription will automatically terminate on August 31st of the current fiscal year. No further charges will be made after this date.

2. Installment Calculation

Your total pledge amount is divided equally by the number of months remaining between your Start Date and August 31st. You are responsible for the full monthly installment amount as displayed in the summary.

3. ACH Payments

If paying via ACH (Bank Transfer), you authorize Stripe to verify your bank account and initiate debits for the agreed amount.

4. Contact

For cancellations or adjustments to your pledge, please contact our administrative office.
